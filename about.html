About Us

Our Story, Our Vision, and Our Faith

Our Story

Shanthi Jeevan Evangelical Mission was founded with a divine calling to serve the "least of these." What started as a small prayer group has grown into a multifaceted ministry touching thousands of lives across the region.

Driven by the compassion of Christ, we stepped out to address not just the spiritual void but also the pressing physical and social needs of our community. From caring for those stigmatized by HIV/AIDS to educating children who had no hope for a future, our journey has been one of faith and miracles.

Our Vision

To see a community transformed by the love of God, where the marginalized find hope, the sick find healing, and every individual discovers their purpose in Christ.

Our Mission

To evangelize the unreached, disciple believers, and demonstrate God's love through holistic care services including medical aid, educational support, and skill development for the underprivileged.
